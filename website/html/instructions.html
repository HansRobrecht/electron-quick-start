Instructions

Controls

  • UP-Arrow | Move Up

  • DOWN-Arrow | Move Down

  • LEFT-Arrow | Move Left

  • RIGHT-Arrow | Move Right

  • ESCAPE | Pauze Game

Game Mechanics

Pointsystem

Each second that you survive the bullethell will earn you a point however this singular point also gets multiplied by the amount of captured zones.

Capturesystem

When standing in a capturezone, it'll slowly transfer from a gray color to a pink gradient. Be warned though if you exit the zone before it finished you'll have to restart the whole process over again.

Lives

You start the game with three lives indicated by hearts in the top right corner, when hit by a projectile this counter will reduce by one. Once you've lost all your hearts the game will end.
